Paige lets you ship production-ready WhatsApp automations by describing what you want in plain language. You chat with the AI to build the bot, test it on real WhatsApp via Paige Dev, and only connect your own WhatsApp number when you're ready to go live. Paige handles the code, the database, the message delivery, and the Meta integration for you.

<Note>
  You can build, iterate, and test your entire bot without ever connecting a WhatsApp number. Connecting WhatsApp is the last step — it just takes you live.
</Note>

## Why use Paige

**Build with natural language.** Describe the behavior you want and the AI writes the bot code. Iterate by chatting — no need to manage a backend, write webhook handlers, or wire up infrastructure.

**Native WhatsApp experiences.** Build interactive multi-screen forms with WhatsApp Flows, send Meta-approved templates, and reach many customers at once with Broadcasts — opt-outs handled for you.

**Everything in one place.** Every project comes with a database, storage for the images and documents your bot sends, scheduled tasks, a place for API keys, connectors for Airtable and Slack, and execution logs — all under the project's **Tools** tab. Your conversation history and full version history sit alongside them.

**Built for teams.** Invite teammates to a shared workspace, collaborate on the same projects, and manage billing in one place.

## Who Paige is for

**Product and operations teams** automating customer support, lead capture, appointment booking, and other recurring conversational workflows.

**Freelancers and agencies** shipping WhatsApp bots for clients without standing up infrastructure for each new project.

**Founders and indie builders** validating ideas on WhatsApp before investing in custom backends.

**Non-technical builders** who want to launch a working bot without writing code, while still being able to drop into the code when they need to.

## What you can build

- Customer support bots that answer FAQs and escalate to humans
- Lead capture flows that qualify prospects and route them to your CRM
- Appointment booking and reminders
- Order tracking, status updates, and post-purchase notifications
- Surveys, feedback collection, and contact forms
- Onboarding sequences with conditional branches
- Internal tools that staff trigger from WhatsApp

## How Paige fits into your workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Create your Paige account at [paigeme.dev](https://paigeme.dev) with email or Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Paige provisions a project with its own bot code, database, and project workspace — no WhatsApp number needed yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build with the AI">
    Describe what you want your bot to do. The AI writes the bot code and you refine it by continuing the conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test with the preview and Paige Dev">
    Try every change in the in-app preview, then test on real WhatsApp via the shared **Paige Dev** number — without ever connecting your own number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect WhatsApp">
    Link your WhatsApp Business number through Meta's embedded signup once your bot is ready for production. Paige manages the webhook and tokens for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy to go live">
    Your first [deploy](/guides/deploy) switches your bot on. Every change after that reaches your customers the same way — so you can keep building and testing safely while your live bot carries on serving.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What it costs

Every new project starts with a **14-day free trial** with the full product unlocked — Conversations, Broadcasts, Deploy, and your bot running on your own number. After that it's a single subscription, plus **credits** for the AI agent's work. See [Billing and credits](/guides/billing).
