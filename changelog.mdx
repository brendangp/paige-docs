Track new features, improvements, and fixes shipped to Paige.

## 2026-05-14

- **Full-access free trial.** Every new project now gets 14 days of full access to Conversations, Broadcasts, Deploy, and bot runtime. After the trial these lock until you subscribe (R570/month) — but the Bot tab, AI chat, Settings, Preview, and the Paige Dev number stay open so you can keep building and connect a WhatsApp number any time. See [Billing](/guides/billing).
- **Preview flows before connecting WhatsApp.** The interactive flow preview ("Try it") in the Flows tab now works as soon as a flow is generated, even if you haven't connected your own WhatsApp number — it falls back to the shared Paige Dev copy. See [Flows Builder](/guides/flows-builder).
