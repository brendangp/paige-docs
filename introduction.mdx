Paige lets you ship production-ready WhatsApp automations by describing what you want in plain language. You connect a WhatsApp Business number, chat with the AI to build the bot, and Paige handles the code, the database, the message delivery, and the Meta integration for you.

## Why use Paige

**Build with natural language.** Describe the behavior you want and the AI writes the bot code. Iterate by chatting — no need to manage a backend, write webhook handlers, or wire up infrastructure.

**Native WhatsApp experiences.** Build interactive multi-screen forms with WhatsApp Flows, send Meta-approved templates, and handle every WhatsApp message type from a single project.

**Everything in one place.** Each project comes with its own bot code editor, database, conversation history, execution logs, secrets manager, and version history.

**Built for teams.** Invite teammates to a shared workspace, collaborate on the same projects, and manage billing in one place.

## Who Paige is for

**Product and operations teams** automating customer support, lead capture, appointment booking, and other recurring conversational workflows.

**Freelancers and agencies** shipping WhatsApp bots for clients without standing up infrastructure for each new project.

**Founders and indie builders** validating ideas on WhatsApp before investing in custom backends.

**Non-technical builders** who want to launch a working bot without writing code, while still being able to drop into the code when they need to.

## What you can build

- Customer support bots that answer FAQs and escalate to humans
- Lead capture flows that qualify prospects and route them to your CRM
- Appointment booking and reminders
- Order tracking, status updates, and post-purchase notifications
- Surveys, feedback collection, and contact forms
- Onboarding sequences with conditional branches
- Internal tools that staff trigger from WhatsApp

## How Paige fits into your workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Sign up at [paigme.dev](https://paigme.dev) and Paige provisions a project with its own bot code, database, and WhatsApp connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect WhatsApp">
    Link your WhatsApp Business number through Meta's embedded signup. Paige manages the webhook and tokens for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build with the AI">
    Describe what you want your bot to do. The AI writes the bot code and you refine it by continuing the conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and deploy">
    Test live with Paige Dev on your own phone, deploy with one click, and roll back to any previous version if something breaks.
  </Step>
</Steps>
